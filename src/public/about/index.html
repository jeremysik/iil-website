Why IsItLegit?

One of the key features of blockchain technology is it's ability to establish consensus between participating parties without the need of trust. Having said this, it's kind of ironic that one of the biggest problems in the crypto space is the legitimacy of some of it's participants/projects.

Every other day you'll hear of scammers running rampart, ripping people off then vanishing into thin air without any means of tracking them down or recovering assets.

I've had a lot of conversations with people that say that web3 will never be a thing because of these scams and bad actors. I refuse to believe this is a problem that we can't solve and IsItLegit? is my attempt to contribute to the solution.
