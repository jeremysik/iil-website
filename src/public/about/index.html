Why IsItLegit?

A very important, hard to solve problem that web3 faces today involves trust, legitimacy and reputation.

Blockchain technology allow us to transact peer-to-peer without fear that the transaction will be tampered with, however, determining who is trustworthy to transact with in the first place is much more complicated.

Token Curated Registries (TCRs), on-chain reputation systems, community project reviews and dox certificates aim to help solve this problem, but there isn't a solution which has reached a decent level of adoption amongst web3 communities yet.

The NFT space is littered with bad actors and rug pulls, members of this community often fall victim and get their assets stolen. The aim of this website is to help the community by shining a spotlight on trustworthy and legitmate NFT projects.
